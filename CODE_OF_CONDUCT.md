# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers of the G.U.I.D.E. Platform pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone, and to uphold Human-Centered AI (HCAI) principles.

## Our Standards

### Positive Behaviors
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Flagging potential ethical concerns in AI development promptly

### Unacceptable Behaviors
- Harassment or discriminatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Introducing AI features that reduce human agency or oversight
- Contributing data or models known to perpetuate harmful biases

## HCAI Ethical Responsibilities

1. **Transparency First:** Never obscure how AI decisions are made.
2. **Bias Awareness:** Actively assess and report potential biases.
3. **Human Oversight:** Never bypass human review for high-stakes decisions.
4. **Privacy by Design:** Treat user data as a responsibility, not a resource.
5. **Charitable Impact:** Uphold the integrity of the philanthropic giving mechanisms.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to **conduct@guide-platform.ai**.

Consequences range from a warning to permanent ban depending on severity.
